Last updated: 01 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Back2Roots (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, dashboard, and related services (the “Service”).

When you sign up and use Back2Roots, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, password, role (student/alumni), college/university, department, and graduation year.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Back2Roots account.
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service and its features.
  • Communicate with you about your account, security alerts, and important updates.
  • Monitor and protect the security and integrity of the Service.
  • Analyse usage to understand how users interact with Back2Roots and to improve user experience.

3. Legal Basis (if applicable)

Depending on your location, we may process your personal data based on your consent, our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Service, and/or the necessity to perform a contract with you (for example, when you create an account and use the platform).

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the Service. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some features.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the Service (for example, hosting or analytics), under appropriate confidentiality and security obligations.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or other change of control, where permitted by applicable law.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We keep your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account as described below.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal requirements.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

9. Children’s Privacy

Back2Roots is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Transfers
